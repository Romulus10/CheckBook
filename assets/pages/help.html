Accounts

Accounts correspond to real-world financial accounts. When you click the "New" button, you will be prompted for a name. The given name creates a new Account object in the application. Accounts have two subsections, Transactions and Categories. Think of transactions as lines in a ledger. Categories are fairly self-explanatory, and allow for easy visualization of how much money you're spending, and planning to spend, on various things.
